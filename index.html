Amazing Cakes

History

Amazing Cakes was founded in 2015 by Susan Smith, who has been passionate about baking since childhood. Anna started baking cakes in her kitchen, selling them to friends and family. Soon, her reputation for making the most delicious cakes delicious and beautiful spread and her small business grew quickly. Over time, Amazing Cakes has become a renowned pastries throughout the region.

Today

Our commitment to quality, creativity and excellent customer service has made us permitted expand and open several branches in different cities. Today, we remain dedicated to our passion for creating cakes exceptional for all types of occasions, from birthdays to weddings. We are grateful for the support of our clients to over the years and we hope to continue sweetening your lives with our incredible cakes.
